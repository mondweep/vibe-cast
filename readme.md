# Practical LLMs and Agentic AI

Guest lecture materials for Greenwich University's Business School, London.

## Overview

This repository contains resources and materials for the guest lecture on practical applications of Large Language Models (LLMs) and Agentic AI in business contexts.

## Topics Covered

- Introduction to Large Language Models
- From LLMs to Agentic AI
- Practical business applications
- Hands-on demonstrations
- Future trends and considerations

## Audience

Business school students and faculty at the University of Greenwich, London.

## Structure

Materials and content will be added as the lecture is developed.
