HBB — Haemoglobin Beta

The E6V variant (Glu→Val at codon 6) causes sickle cell disease — the most common single-gene disorder worldwide. A single A→T transversion at coding position 20 is responsible. The haemoglobin protein polymerises, distorting red blood cells into their characteristic sickle shape.

First 37 coding bases — position 20 (mutation site) highlighted in red:

ATGGTGCACCTGACTTCCATGTGAGCTGCACGTGGAT ← pos 20: A→T causes E6V

Protein translation: Met-Val-His-Leu-Thr-Pro-Glu-Glu-Lys-Ser... (Val replaces Glu at position 6)

TP53 — Tumour Protein p53

The "guardian of the genome" — mutated in over 50% of all human cancers. Acts as a transcription factor triggering apoptosis or cell-cycle arrest in response to DNA damage. Its 20-kb coding sequence spans 11 exons.

Cosine similarity with BRCA1: 0.498

TP53 and BRCA1 cooperate in the DNA damage signalling pathway. Their shared k-mer patterns reflect overlapping regulatory motifs — explaining their proximity in the 512D sequence space.

TP53/CYP2D6 similarity: 0.461

Highest cross-function pair. Both respond to cellular stress: TP53 to genotoxic stress, CYP2D6 to xenobiotic/drug exposure. Shared stress-response sequence motifs drive the similarity.

BRCA1 — Breast Cancer Susceptibility Gene 1

BRCA1 185delAG carriers face a 52% lifetime breast cancer risk and 39% ovarian cancer risk by age 70. The platform models this trajectory using temporal attractor dynamics with FTLE-derived confidence bands.

P(carrier | positive test) ≈ 22%

With 95% sensitivity, 99% specificity, and 0.3% population prevalence, Bayes' theorem gives P(carrier|+) = 0.95×0.003 / (0.95×0.003 + 0.01×0.997) ≈ 0.222. Most positive tests are false positives — a critical lesson in probabilistic reasoning.

CYP2D6 — Cytochrome P450 2D6

Responsible for metabolising approximately 25% of all prescribed drugs. Over 100 star alleles documented. The diplotype (*1/*4, *4/*4 etc.) determines metaboliser phenotype, directly driving dosing decisions.

// src/api.rs — star allele phenotype mapping fn predict_phenotype(diplotype: &str) -> Phenotype { match diplotype { "*1/*1" | "*1/*2" => Phenotype::Normal, "*1/*4" | "*1/*5" => Phenotype::Intermediate, "*4/*4" | "*4/*5" => Phenotype::PoorMetaboliser, "*1xN/*1" => Phenotype::UltraRapid, _ => Phenotype::Unknown, } }

INS — Insulin

The insulin gene underpins the T2D risk trajectory. Promoter variants affect beta-cell function. The platform models age-dependent T2D risk (11% at 30 → 53% at 70) via temporal attractor dynamics with FTLE confidence bands.

HNSW similarity: INS vs BRCA1 = 0.380

Lowest similarity in the panel. INS is metabolic/endocrine while BRCA1 is nuclear/repair — different biological contexts produce distinct k-mer landscapes, far apart in sequence space.

Layer 4 — Savant AI

Bayesian Prior Updating

Each genomic trait stores a Beta(alpha, beta) distribution. Click the observation buttons to watch the posterior update in real time — exactly what Savant AI does internally.

P(H | D) ∝ P(D | H) × P(H)

Posterior is proportional to Likelihood times Prior. The Beta distribution is the natural conjugate prior for Bernoulli observations: each pathogenic observation increments alpha, each benign increments beta. The distribution narrows as evidence accumulates.

Layer 6

SAFLA Safety Framework

No clinical recommendation leaves the platform without passing six validation checks. Every decision is auditable, explainable, and overridable by a clinician.

🎯
Confidence Threshold
Bayesian posterior must exceed 0.75. Below threshold → flagged for clinician review, not emitted.
⚖️
Contradiction Detection
Cross-references all panel genes for conflicting signals. Conflict → escalation to multi-expert advisory panel.
📋
Regulatory Classification
Determines CE-IVD / FDA 510(k) classification. Risk stratified per IVD Regulation (EU) 2017/746.
🔍
Explainability Check
Every recommendation must trace back to specific base positions. Black-box outputs are rejected automatically.
🚨
Critical Drug Interaction
Scans curated interaction database. Life-threatening interactions trigger a hard block — no recommendation emitted.
📝
Audit Trail Generation
Immutable record: hashed sequences, variant calls, confidence scores, model versions, timestamp. GDPR Article 22 compliant.
Technology

Open-Source Stack

Every component is open-source, built to be studied, extended, and forked.

🦀

Rust Backend

Axum + Tokio async runtime. Server-Sent Events streaming API. High-performance DNA parsing via the rvdna crate at 3.2 bits per base.

⚛️

Next.js 16 Frontend

React 19, HeroUI components, Recharts for data visualisation, Three.js for 3D molecular structures, Framer Motion for animations.

🧠

ruv-FANN Neural Nets

Rust-native feed-forward neural networks. Classifies 512D k-mer vectors into five pathogenicity classes end-to-end.

🔍

RuVector HNSW

Hierarchical Navigable Small World approximate nearest-neighbour search. Sub-millisecond gene similarity queries across the full 512D vector space.

📊

Savant AI Bayesian

Beta distribution per genomic trait. Conjugate prior updates with each new variant observation. Uncertainty quantification at every step.

🛡️

SAFLA Safety

Six-layer validation pipeline: confidence gating, regulatory classification, explainability mandates, interaction checking, immutable audit trails.
