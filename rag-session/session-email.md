## What is RAG?

Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) is a technique that enhances Large Language Models by allowing them to access and utilise external knowledge sources. This addresses key limitations of LLMs, such as outdated training data and hallucinations, by grounding responses in relevant, retrieved documents.
