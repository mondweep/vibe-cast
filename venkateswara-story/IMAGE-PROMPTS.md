## Scene 1: Vaikuntha - The Eternal Abode

**Prompt:**
```
A majestic scene in Vaikuntha (heavenly abode), traditional Tanjore painting style.
Lord Vishnu with blue skin, four arms holding conch, discus, mace, and lotus,
reclining peacefully on the coiled serpent Adishesha with multiple hoods forming
a canopy. Goddess Lakshmi in red and gold saree, pressing his feet devotedly.
The setting is a golden palace with pillars, celestial clouds, divine light rays.
Rich gold leaf details, vibrant colors, ornate jewelry, serene expressions.
Decorative border with lotus motifs. Devotional Hindu art style, highly detailed.
```

**Dimensions:** 16:9 landscape
**Key Elements:** Vishnu (blue), Adishesha (serpent), Lakshmi (red/gold), golden palace

## Scene 2: Sage Bhrigu's Journey

**Prompt:**
```
Traditional Indian miniature painting showing Sage Bhrigu's spiritual journey.
Three panels or a flowing scene: (1) Bhrigu approaching Lord Brahma on a lotus
in Brahmaloka, (2) Bhrigu at Mount Kailash with Lord Shiva meditating,
(3) Bhrigu entering the gates of Vaikuntha. The sage has matted hair, beard,
simple saffron robes, carrying a staff. Each realm has distinct characteristics -
Brahmaloka with lotuses, Kailash with snow mountains, Vaikuntha with golden light.
Pahari miniature style, rich colors, gold accents, divine atmospheres.
```

**Dimensions:** 16:9 landscape or 3:1 panoramic
**Key Elements:** Bhrigu (saffron robes), three realms, spiritual journey

## Scene 3: The Fateful Kick (Bhrigu Kicks Vishnu)

**Prompt:**
```
Dramatic moment in traditional Kangra/Pahari painting style. Sage Bhrigu with
fierce expression, foot raised, kicking the chest of Lord Vishnu who lies on
Adishesha. Goddess Lakshmi watches in shock and anger, her hand raised in dismay.
Vishnu's expression is calm and forgiving despite the insult. The scene captures
the tension - Lakshmi's Srivatsa mark (her abode on Vishnu's chest) being violated.
Rich royal blue for Vishnu, red and gold for Lakshmi, earthy tones for Bhrigu.
Golden light emanating from the scene. Historic Indian manuscript painting style.
```

**Dimensions:** 4:3 or 1:1 square
**Key Elements:** Bhrigu's foot on Vishnu's chest, shocked Lakshmi, calm Vishnu

## Image Generation Tips for Gemini

1. **Consistency**: Generate all images in one session if possible to maintain style consistency
2. **Seed/Reference**: If Gemini supports it, use the first image as a style reference for others
3. **Aspect Ratios**: Match the recommended dimensions for each scene
4. **Iterations**: Generate 3-4 variations and pick the best
5. **Quality**: Request highest quality/resolution available
6. **Cultural Accuracy**: Gemini should recognize Hindu iconographic elements - if not accurate, specify details more explicitly
