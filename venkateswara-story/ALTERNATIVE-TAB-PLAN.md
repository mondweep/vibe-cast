# Communication Plan: Alternative Perspectives Tab
## Visual Storytelling for Hypothetical Research

---

## Overview

Create a second tab on `index.html` that presents the alternative/hypothetical perspectives in an engaging, visually-driven format. The tone should be:
- **Intellectually curious** (not dismissive or sensationalist)
- **Clearly labeled as speculative** (disclaimers throughout)
- **Visually distinct** from the devotional tab (different color scheme)
- **Interactive and explorative** (encouraging thought, not belief)

---

## Navigation & UX

### Tab Behavior
- Smooth fade transition between tabs
- URL hash for direct linking (`#sacred`, `#alternative`)
- Remember last viewed tab in localStorage
- Clear visual indicator of active tab

### Disclaimer Placement
- Floating "HYPOTHETICAL" badge on alternative tab
- Section-level reminders
- Footer disclaimer

### Cross-linking
- "Return to Sacred Story" button in alternative tab
- "Explore Alternative Perspectives" link in sacred story (subtle)

---

## Responsive Considerations

### Mobile
- Tabs become full-width buttons
- Stacked comparison cards
- Simplified imagery (single images vs splits)

### Tablet
- Side-by-side comparisons work
- Reduced animation for performance

### Desktop
- Full visual experience
- Parallax effects on section backgrounds
- Hover states on comparison cards
