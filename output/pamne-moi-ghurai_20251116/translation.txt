ASSAMESE LYRICS & ENGLISH TRANSLATION
================================================================================

1. Paamone moi ghurai
   [Will I get it back?]

2. Akou ebaar jibonot
   [Once again in life,]

3. Deutaar chokute chai howa sei chobi
   [That image (of me), reflected in my father's eyes.]

4. Hiyar aamthu
   [The treasure of my heart.]

5. Bhabi than baan hole
   [When (my mind) shatters from all the thinking,]

6. Jironi lobole laage chage ekhoni buku
   [Perhaps one needs a bosom (heart) to take rest.]

7. Etiyao dehot laagi ase subas
   [The fragrance still lingers on my body,]

8. Dukhoni hator umal porosh
   [The warm touch of those two hands.]

9. Kiyo jano hothate dithok hol sopon
   [I don't know why the dream suddenly became real,]

10. Kakhote thakio nopuwar bedona
    [The pain of not having, even while being so close.]

11. Somukhot dekhio nedekhar jatona
    [The agony of not seeing, even while looking right at it.]

12. Koneno bujibo kaar buku eka dibo
    [Who will understand whose heart is being torn apart?]

13. Paamone moi ghurai (Repeat)
    [Will I get it back?]

14. Akou ebaar jibonot (Repeat)
    [Once again in life,]

15. Deutaar chokut chai howa sei chobi
    [That picture (of me), seen in my father's eyes.]
