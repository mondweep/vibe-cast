# Cover Letter Template

**Use this structure for role-specific cover letters**

---

## Template Structure

### Opening Paragraph (1 para)
Hook with role + your differentiator

**Template:**
> Dear [Hiring Manager],
> 
> I am writing to express my strong interest in the [Role Title] position at [Company]. With [X years] of [relevant experience], I'm confident I can [specific value for this role].

**Example (Mondweep for PwC):**
> Dear PwC Hiring Team,
> 
> I am writing to express my strong interest in the Director of AI Engineering position within PwC's Tech Catalyst UK practice. I've previously collaborated with PwC colleagues on large-scale digital transformations, and I'm excited about the opportunity to join the firm and help drive your clients' AI-enabled modernisation at scale.

---

### Body Paragraph 1: Experience & Track Record (1-2 para)
Your specific experience relevant to this role

**Template:**
> My background combines [Key 1] + [Key 2] + [Key 3]. I've [Achievement 1], [Achievement 2], and [Achievement 3]. This blend of [differentiator] is what separates truly effective [role] leaders.

**Example:**
> My background is precisely what Tech Catalyst needs: 20 years of enterprise transformation leadership combined with deep hands-on AI engineering capability. I've architected and contributed to 40+ AI systems across financial services (HSBC, Barclays, Zafin, MUFG), telecommunications (Shell Energy, TalkTalk), and automotive (JLR, Lotus). I've led multi-million-pound programs delivering measurable business outcomes—£150M+ in revenue unlock (Lotus), £120M+ in customer migration (Shell Energy), £100M+ in legacy modernization (JLR).

---

### Body Paragraph 2: Why This Role + Company (1 para)
Show you understand their mission and why you're passionate

**Template:**
> What I find compelling about [Company]/[Role] is [specific insight about their work]. Your [specific initiative/focus] aligns directly with my [specific expertise]. I would bring [specific capability] to help [specific business outcome].

**Example:**
> Beyond the numbers, I understand the consulting delivery model: stakeholder management across client organizations, translating strategic vision into executable roadmaps, building and scaling delivery teams, and maintaining quality and discipline at pace. I've done this at Wipro Digital and throughout my career, and I bring both the technical credibility and the program management discipline that define effective leadership in the consulting space.

---

### Closing Paragraph (1 para)
Call to action + enthusiasm

**Template:**
> I would be excited to discuss how my [X years] of [experience] and [specific capability] can drive [specific outcome] for [Company]. Please feel free to reach out at [email/phone].
> 
> Best regards,  
> [Your Name]

**Example:**
> I would be excited to bring this expertise to PwC's Tech Catalyst UK and help drive transformational impact for your clients. I'm happy to discuss how my 20 years of enterprise transformation delivery and hands-on AI engineering can help the firm scale its AI services practice.
> 
> Best regards,  
> Mondweep Chakravorty

---

## Length & Tone

- **Length:** 3-4 paragraphs (max 1 page)
- **Tone:** Executive, professional, confident (not salesy)
- **Language:** Business-focused (not jargon-heavy)

---

## Checklist

- [ ] Opening hooks with role + differentiator
- [ ] Body shows specific experience + track record
- [ ] Demonstrates understanding of role/company
- [ ] Quantified achievements included
- [ ] Calls to action is clear
- [ ] No typos or grammatical errors
- [ ] Personalized to role + company (not generic)

---

## Do's & Don'ts

✅ **Do:**
- Personalize every letter (different for each role)
- Lead with value (what you deliver, not what you want)
- Use specific metrics (£, %, time)
- Reference their recent work/news (shows research)
- Keep to 1 page

❌ **Don't:**
- Use template language ("I am passionate about...")
- Make it about your needs (salary, flex, location)
- Go longer than 1 page
- Repeat your CV
- Use generic "Dear Hiring Manager"
