# Step 2: Positioning (Days 3-5)

**Objective:** Develop buyer-focused positioning and executive summary. Create 3-5 role-specific versions.

---

## Positioning Framework

### Core Formula

**"I help [Buyer] at [Company Type] achieve [Business Outcome] through [Unique Approach]."**

**Example (Mondweep):**
> "I help CIOs at global organisations achieve revenue growth in excess of £15M+ through enterprise AI transformation. With 20 years of delivery excellence, I've shaped technology strategies that realised £150M+ in new business (Lotus), £120M+ in customer migration and retention (Shell Energy), £100M+ in legacy modernisation (JLR). I have architected and contributed to 40+ AI projects and systems and led implementation of responsible governance frameworks."

---

## Part A: Buyer-Specific Positioning (30 min)

Create 3-5 versions, each emphasizing different buyer priorities:

### Version A: CIO / Digital Transformation
**Focus:** Strategy + execution, digital modernization, IT modernization, board-level impact

**Template:**
> I help CIOs navigate digital transformation by architecting and executing enterprise AI systems. With [X years] at [key firms], I've delivered [£X in impact] through cloud modernization, legacy transformation, and responsible governance.

### Version B: Consulting / Client Delivery
**Focus:** Client outcomes, program delivery, multi-sector experience, business case development

**Template:**
> I help consulting firms scale their AI practice by bringing both client delivery discipline and hands-on engineering capability. With [consulting background] and [X years] delivering at scale, I combine strategic clarity with execution rigor.

### Version C: CFO / Financial Impact
**Focus:** Revenue, cost savings, risk mitigation, financial outcomes

**Template:**
> I help finance leaders unlock value through AI-enabled transformation. With [X years] in financial services and [£X impact], I've delivered measurable ROI through strategic roadmaps and disciplined execution.

### Version D: COO / Operations & Efficiency
**Focus:** Scale, efficiency, operations, team leadership, program management

**Template:**
> I help COOs scale operations through technology modernization and AI. With [X years] managing [scale], I've delivered [efficiency gain] through organisational transformation and program leadership.

### Version E: VP Product / Innovation
**Focus:** Innovation, speed to market, product strategy, competitive advantage

**Template:**
> I help product leaders accelerate innovation through responsible AI. With [X years] architecting [innovations] and [experience], I combine cutting-edge thinking with delivery discipline.

## Part C: Role-Specific Positioning Docs (1.5 hours)

Create a full positioning doc for each of your top 3-5 target roles.

### Template Structure

**[Role] @ [Company Type] – Positioning Document**

1. **Buyer Profile** (2-3 sentences)
   - Who you're talking to
   - Their challenges
   - What they care about

2. **Your Differentiation** (3-4 bullets)
   - Your unique approach
   - Your track record
   - Your credibility
   - Your values/principles

3. **Proof Points** (3-5 bullets)
   - Named achievements
   - Named clients/companies
   - Quantified results
   - Relevant credentials

4. **Positioning Statement** (2-3 sentences)
   - How you'll help them
   - What sets you apart
   - Why you're the right fit

5. **Call to Action** (1 sentence)
   - What you want to happen next

### Example: PwC Tech Catalyst UK

**Director of AI Engineering @ PwC Consulting – Positioning Document**

1. **Buyer Profile**
   - Global consulting firm scaling AI services practice
   - Needs technical leaders who can deliver client value at scale
   - Cares about: client outcomes, team delivery, business case ROI

2. **Your Differentiation**
   - 20 years enterprise transformation (not just tech)
   - Proven consulting delivery model (Wipro Digital experience)
   - Architect who executes (not advisor)
   - Deep financial services experience

3. **Proof Points**
   - Delivered £150M+ in revenue (Lotus marketplace)
   - 40+ AI systems architected and deployed
   - Managed £50M+ budgets, 300+ person teams
   - Financial services across 6 major institutions
   - Responsible AI governance (Agentics Foundation co-lead)

4. **Positioning Statement**
   - You combine the technical credibility and consulting discipline PwC needs
   - You understand both strategy and execution
   - You can build and scale high-performing delivery teams
   - You bring client-centric thinking

5. **Call to Action**
   - Let's discuss how I can help Tech Catalyst scale AI delivery
