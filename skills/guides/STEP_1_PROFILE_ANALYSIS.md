## Part D: Buyer Persona Identification (15 min)

**Who are you really helping?**

Define your primary buyer:

### Exercise: Fill in

- **Title:** (CIO, CFO, COO, VP Product, etc.)
- **Company size:** (Enterprise, mid-market, consulting)
- **Sectors:** (Finance, tech, healthcare, etc.)
- **Challenge they face:** (Digital transformation, cost reduction, competitive advantage)
- **What they care about:** (Revenue, risk, efficiency, innovation, governance)
- **Decision criteria:** (ROI, risk mitigation, team capability, timeline)

### Example (Mondweep)
- **Buyer:** CIO
- **Company:** Global organization, £500M+ revenue
