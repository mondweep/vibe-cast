About Me

With 20 years of delivery excellence across enterprise transformation, cloud modernisation, and AI-enabled innovation, I specialise in architecting and executing technology strategies that unlock measurable business value.

My background spans 13 years in financial services (HSBC, Barclays, SEB, Zafin, MUFG, Lloyds), 4 years in utilities and telecommunications (Shell Energy, TalkTalk), and 3 years in automotive manufacturing (JLR, Lotus).
