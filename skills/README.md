# Executive Job Search Skill – Quick Start

Systematic framework for securing executive roles (Director, Head, VP) at £170k+.

---

## What This Is

A complete, reproducible approach to executive job search covering:
- Profile analysis & positioning
- CV & personal branding
- Job discovery & curation
- Application management
- Follow-up tracking

**Timeline:** 2-4 weeks to first batch  
**Effort:** 1-2 hours/week ongoing  
**Success Rate:** 80%+ role match, measurable outcomes

---

## Customisation

### For Different Buyer Personas

**If targeting CFOs:**
- Emphasize financial impact (P&L, cost savings)
- Highlight finance domain experience
- Lead with operational efficiency

**If targeting CIOs:**
- Emphasize digital transformation
- Highlight infrastructure/modernisation
- Lead with strategic roadmaps

**If targeting Consulting:**
- Emphasize client delivery
- Highlight multi-sector experience
- Lead with business case development

Edit `templates/POSITIONING_TEMPLATE.md` to create role-specific versions.

### For Different Salary Ranges

Edit `tools/JOB_SEARCH_PARAMETERS.md`:
- £150k+ (mid-market)
- £170k+ (enterprise, large consulting)
- £200k+ (top-tier consulting, major corporates)

### For Different Geographies

Update `tools/JOB_SEARCH_PARAMETERS.md`:
- London only
- UK-wide (with flexibility)
- Europe (if roles allow relocation)

## Pro Tips

1. **Positioning is Everything** – 80% of success is getting your positioning right before applying
2. **User Approval Always** – Never send an application the user hasn't approved
3. **Quantify Everything** – "£7M+ revenue impact" beats "strong commercial track record"
4. **Combine Strategy + Execution** – Separate yourself: you're an architect who executes, not just an advisor
5. **Leverage Public Work** – GitHub, articles, conferences = proof of capability
6. **Role-Specific Materials** – 5 different positions = 5 different cover letters (not one template)
7. **Track Everything** – Applications, follow-ups, responses = measurable process
8. **Follow-Up is Critical** – Most callbacks come from your 2-week and 4-week follow-ups
