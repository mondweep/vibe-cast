## 🧭 Navigational Coordinates

### I. The Nature of the Path
- **Interface**: The body is a temple (Microcosm = Macrocosm).
- **Engine**: Shakti (Primordial Energy) and Shiva (Pure Awareness).
- **Media**: Mantra (Sound), Yantra (Geometry), Ritual (Action).

### II. The Two Wings (The Paths)
- **Dakshina Marg (Right-Hand Path)**: The path of purity, external ritual, and structured ascent. Safe and aligned with social/Vedic norms.
- **Vama Marg (Left-Hand Path)**: The path of intensive energy transformation, utilizing "taboo" substances and unconventional methods to shock the system into awakening.
- **The Direct Path**: Transcendence of both wings into non-dual recognition.

### III. The Dasha Mahavidyas (The 10 Wisdom Perspectives)
These are not just "Goddesses," but ten unique ways consciousness views and realizes itself:
1. **Kali**: The Power of Time and Transformation.
2. **Tara**: The Power of Sound and Saving Word.
3. **Tripura Sundari**: The Power of Pure Perception and Beauty.
4. **Bhuvaneshvari**: The Power of Space and Infinite Vision.
5. **Bhairavi**: The Power of Tapas (Fierce Discipline).
6. **Chhinnamasta**: The Power of Consciousness Beyond Mind.
7. **Dhumavati**: The Power of the Void and Primordial Darkness.
8. **Bagalamukhi**: The Power of Silence and Reversal of Opposites.
9. **Matangi**: The Power of Spoken Word and Art.
10. **Kamalatmika**: The Power of Delight and Spiritual Abundance.
