## ⚡ The Dasha Mahavidyas: 10 Perspectives of Reality
These are the ten "Wisdom Forms" that represent different ways the Divine Mother views the play of existence.

### 1-5: The Primal Force
- **Kali (Time)**: The power of transformation. She represents the "Engine" of the UPS.
- **Tara (The Saving Word)**: The power of Om and the Breath. She is the "Navigator."
- **Tripura Sundari (Beauty)**: The power of pure perception. The "Vibe" of the field.
- **Bhuvaneshvari (Space)**: The power of the infinite vision. The "Context" or "Repository."
- **Bhairavi (Tapas)**: The power of fierce discipline. The "Constraint" that forces breakthrough.

### 6-10: The Subtle Dynamics
- **Chhinnamasta (Beyond Mind)**: The image of the severed head; going beyond binary thought.
- **Dhumavati (The Void)**: The power of suffering and the grandmother spirit. The "Shadow" worker.
- **Bagalamukhi (Silence)**: The power to stop movement and reverse opposites. The "Stoppage" protocol.
- **Matangi (Spoken Word)**: The power of art and the guru. The "Scribe" and "Communicator."
- **Kamalatmika (Delight)**: The power of abundance and spiritual health. The "Harvest."
