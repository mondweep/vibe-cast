# 🧘 Ritual & Practice: Operational Transformation
*Distilled from David Frawley's "Tantric Yoga"*

## 🏗️ The Multi-Layered Interface (The 3 Bodies)
The human being is not a single entity, but a set of nested "bodies" or vehicles for consciousness.

| Body | Sanskrit Name | Nature | Function |
|------|---------------|--------|----------|
| **Gross** | *Sthula Sharira* | Physical | Waking state; built by food; biological humors (Vata, Pitta, Kapha). |
| **Subtle** | *Sukshma Sharira* | Astral | Dream/Trance; built by Prana; seat of the 7 Chakras and Nadis. |
| **Causal** | *Karana Sharira* | Karmic | Deep sleep; seat of Ego and Intelligence; stores seeds of karma. |

---

## 🎡 The 7 Chakras (Energy Coordinates)
These are not physical locations, but vibratory centers in the subtle body.

1. **Earth (Root)**: Foundation and ego-grounding.
2. **Water (Sex)**: Energy flow and absorption.
3. **Fire (Navel)**: Perception and transformative reason (Buddhi).
4. **Air (Heart)**: Feeling and conditioned consciousness (Citta).
5. **Ether (Throat)**: The source of individual soul (Jiva) and space.
6. **Third Eye (Mind)**: The Command Center (Ajna) for cosmic contact.
7. **Crown (Head)**: The seat of Pure Spirit (Atman/Purusha).

---

## 🌬️ The Pranic Engine (The 5 Vayus)
For the UPS to navigate, it needs "fuel" (Vital Airs).

- **Samana**: Equalizing, creates space and equanimity (Heart/Navel).
- **Vyana**: Diffusive, gives comprehension and circulation (Whole body).
- **Udana**: **Ascending**, the power that moves energy up the spine (Throat/Fire).
- **Prana**: Inward-looking, allows absorption of sensations (Heart).
- **Apana**: Downward-moving, warding off obstacles and elimination (Root).

---

## 🛣️ The 3 Nadis (Navigational Channels)
- **Sushumna (Fire)**: The central void. True navigation only happens here.
- **Ida (Moon/Left)**: Cooling, emotional, white.
- **Pingala (Sun/Right)**: Heating, logical, red.

## 💎 The Direct Mastery (Section Highlight)
Frawley identifies the **Amrita Nadi** (The Immortal Channel). 
> "All that yogis accomplish through taking the Kundalini up through the seven chakras can be experienced by... dwelling on the spiritual heart alone."

This is the **Sovereignty Bypass**—recognizing the field as yourself without needing to map each individual point of resistance.
