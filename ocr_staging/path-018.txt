PART I

TANTRA RE-EXAMINED

The Goddess Speaks:

Before the beginning of the universe I
alone existed, with nothing other than my-
self. That Self-nature is called by the names
of consciousness, wisdom, and the supreme
Brahman. — Devi Gita IV.3

I create the Father of the universe on the
summit of the worlds. My origin is within
the cosmic waters, in the universal sea.
From there I extend to all the worlds and
touch the ridge of Heaven.

I blow like the wind, setting in motion all
the universe. Far beyond Heaven and
beyond this Earth extends my greatness.

— Vak Ambhrini, Rig Veda X.125.7-8
