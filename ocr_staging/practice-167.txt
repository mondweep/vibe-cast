168 — Tantric Yoga and the Wisdom Goddesses —
5. Vishuddha Chakra — Bther Chakra (Throat)

The Ether Chakra is often called the throat center. Vishuddha means
“very pure,” as this center is very subtle.

The Ether Chakra has sixteen petals consisting of the vowels of the
Sanskrit alphabet: am, am, im, im, um, iim, rm, fm, Irm, Irm, em, aim,
om, aum, am, and ah. It is the seat of space, the element of ether or the
etheric state of matter, whose governing seed syllable is Ham.

The Yogini governing this chakra is Dakini, and among the seven
tissues it relates to the plasma.

It rules the organ of sound, the ears, and the vocal organ (mouth)
among the organs of action.

Itis the site of the individual soul (jiva) and is the source of inspiration.
It is the seat of the balancing air or vital force (Samana Vayu).

In the physical body it relates to the throat and larynx and to the
respiratory system. In the endocrine system its counterpart is the thyroid.

Its elemental symbol is a dark blue circle. The color of the chakra
itself is a smoky grey.
